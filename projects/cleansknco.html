Project Overview

View Live Store GitHub Repository

Challenge

Create a lead generation email funnel for CleanSkin Co, a clean skincare brand targeting customers interested in simple, effective skincare routines.

Solution

Designed and developed a 3-email welcome series with lead magnet delivery, built using MJML for maximum compatibility and integrated with Klaviyo for automation.

Tools Used

MJML Klaviyo Shopify CSS

Timeline

3 weeks - Setup, email design, flow creation, and testing

Email Templates

Email 1: Welcome + Guide Delivery

Purpose: Deliver the free skincare guide and set expectations for the email series.

Key Elements: Brand introduction, guide download link, clean design matching store aesthetic.

Open Rate: 68% Click Rate: 12%

Email 2: Why Simple Skincare Works

Purpose: Educate subscribers about the benefits of simplified skincare routines.

Key Elements: Educational content, trust building, subtle product positioning.

Open Rate: 45% Click Rate: 8%

Email 3: Meet Your Starter Kit

Purpose: Soft introduction to the 3-product starter kit with clear value proposition.

Key Elements: Product showcase, benefits-focused copy, clear CTA to browse products.

Open Rate: 38% Click Rate: 15%

Email Flow Structure

The welcome series triggers when users sign up for the free skincare guide, delivering value-first content before introducing products.
