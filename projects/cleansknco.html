Project Overview

Challenge

Create lead generation email funnel for clean skincare brand with free guide as lead magnet targeting simple, effective skincare routines.

Solution

3-email welcome series with educational content and product introduction, built using MJML for maximum compatibility and Klaviyo automation.

Results

58% average open rate, 17% click rate, 48% flow completion rate across the campaign.

Timeline

3 weeks - Store setup, email design, automation flows, and testing.
