Challenge

Create a lead generation email funnel for CleanSkn Co, a clean skincare brand targeting customers interested in simple, effective skincare routines with a free guide as lead magnet.

Solution

Designed and developed a 3-email welcome series with educational content and product introduction, built using MJML for maximum compatibility and integrated with Klaviyo automation.

Tools Used

MJML Klaviyo Shopify HTML/CSS

Timeline

3 weeks - Store setup, email design, automation flows, and testing

Email Campaign Series

Email 1: Welcome + Guide Delivery

Purpose: Welcome new subscribers and deliver the free 5-step skincare routine guide immediately.

Key Elements: Brand introduction, guide download CTA, skincare benefits overview.

Open Rate: 72% Click Rate: 18%

Email 2: Why Clean Skin Co. Products Actually Work

Purpose: Build trust through science-backed skincare education and ingredient transparency.

Key Elements: Ingredient benefits, plant-powered approach, no-fluff messaging.

Open Rate: 58% Click Rate: 12%

Email 3: St.Ives Face Scrub Introduction

Purpose: Introduce specific product with customer testimonials and gentle sales approach.

Key Elements: Product hero image, customer reviews, feature callouts, soft CTA.

Open Rate: 45% Click Rate: 22%

Shopify Store Integration

Complete Customer Journey

  • Landing Page: Lead magnet capture with compelling offer
  • Email Signup: Optimized popup for maximum conversions
  • Klaviyo Integration: Automated flow triggers based on customer actions
  • Product Pages: Clean, conversion-focused design

Email Automation Flow

The welcome series triggers when users sign up for the free skincare guide, delivering educational value before introducing products through a strategic soft-sell approach.
