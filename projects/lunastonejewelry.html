Challenge

Design a comprehensive email marketing suite for Luna & Stone Jewelry, a handcrafted jewelry brand focused on gemstone meanings and personal connection. Create multiple automation flows to nurture customers throughout their journey.

Solution

Developed 5 distinct email campaigns including welcome series, educational content, abandoned cart recovery, and customer retention flows. Built custom MJML templates with Klaviyo automation integration.

Tools Used

MJML Klaviyo Shopify HTML/CSS Email Strategy

Timeline

4 weeks - Complete email system design, development, automation setup, and performance optimization

Email Campaign Series

Email 1: Welcome + Brand Story

Purpose: Introduce new subscribers to Sarah's story and Luna & Stone's handcrafted approach with personal brand narrative.

Key Elements: Personal welcome from Sarah, behind-the-scenes story, brand values, 15% welcome discount.

Open Rate: 68% Click Rate: 24%

Email 2: The Magic Behind Your Stones

Purpose: Educate subscribers about gemstone meanings, properties, and care instructions to build expertise and trust.

Key Elements: Gemstone guide (moonstone, rose quartz, amethyst, labradorite), care tips, stone selection philosophy.

Open Rate: 61% Click Rate: 19%

Email 3: Why Our Community Loves Luna & Stone

Purpose: Showcase customer testimonials, reviews, and community love to build trust and encourage first purchase.

Key Elements: Customer testimonials, 4.9★ rating showcase, Instagram social proof, final discount reminder.

Open Rate: 52% Click Rate: 31%

Automation Flows

Cart Recovery 1: Still Thinking About Your Pieces?

Timing: 3 hours after cart abandonment

Approach: Gentle reminder with personal message from Sarah, cart item display, 24-hour reservation urgency.

Open Rate: 45% Recovery Rate: 12%

Cart Recovery 2: Only a Few Hours Left

Timing: 2 days after first email

Approach: Higher urgency with 10% discount offer, scarcity messaging, social proof testimonial.

Open Rate: 38% Recovery Rate: 18%

Klaviyo Automation Setup

Welcome series triggers when users sign up for the newsletter, delivering personal brand story, education, and social proof over 6 days to build trust before purchase.

Abandoned cart sequence activates when checkout is started but not completed, using escalating urgency and discount incentives to recover lost sales.

Shopify Store Integration

Complete E-commerce Experience

  • Homepage Design: Mystical jewelry aesthetic with clear email signup integration
  • Product Collections: Organized by jewelry type (rings, necklaces, earrings, bracelets)
  • Email Capture: Multiple touchpoints for newsletter signup with 10% incentive
  • Klaviyo Integration: Seamless data flow between store and email platform
