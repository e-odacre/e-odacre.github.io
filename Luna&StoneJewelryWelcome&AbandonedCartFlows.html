Luna & Stone Jewelry – Welcome & Abandoned Cart Flows

Building personalized email journeys with MJML and Klaviyo.

Overview

For Luna & Stone Jewelry, a Shopify-based brand, I built both the storefront and customer communication system from the ground up. After setting up the Shopify store and customizing the theme with Liquid, I integrated Klaviyo to design two automated flows:

  • Welcome Flow (3 emails): Welcome, Education/Product Story, Social Proof with Reviews.
  • Abandoned Cart Flow (2 emails): Reminder and Last Chance urgency email.

All emails were coded in MJML for responsiveness, tested across devices, and styled to align with the brand’s elegant aesthetic.

Process

  • Built a Shopify store and added product catalog with a custom pop-up form for email capture.
  • Coded branded MJML templates with personalized details and customer review blocks.
  • Planned multi-step flows that combined storytelling, education, and urgency.
  • Set up automation in Klaviyo with triggers, timing, and segmentation.
  • QA tested across desktop, mobile, and popular inboxes to ensure consistency.

Results & Learnings

  • Delivered a complete customer journey with automated flows.
  • Learned how to blend personalization with automation (e.g., using customer reviews for social proof).
  • Improved efficiency and confidence coding MJML compared to my first project.
  • Gained experience building an email + Shopify ecosystem from scratch.

What I learned: Campaigns thrive when they balance design, messaging, and segmentation — and small A/B test improvements compound into major results.
