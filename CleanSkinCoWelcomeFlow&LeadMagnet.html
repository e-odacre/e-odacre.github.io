CleanSkin Co. – Welcome Flow & Lead Magnet

Turning new subscribers into engaged customers with education and design.

Overview

For CleanSkin Co., a Shopify skincare brand, I built a storefront from a template and customized it with Liquid to match a clean, minimal aesthetic. To build trust immediately, I added a pop-up offering a free 5-step skincare guide. This became the centerpiece of a nurturing 3-email welcome flow:

  • Welcome Email + Free Guide — value-first introduction.
  • Educational Email — skincare tips framed with product context.
  • Soft Sell Email — gentle promotion without pressure.

These emails combined polished copy, elegant design, and user-focused storytelling—making them one of my most refined projects.

Process

  • Built and customized a Shopify storefront using Liquid.
  • Designed a lead magnet popup offering the skincare guide.
  • Coded responsive MJML email templates for the entire flow.
  • Balanced education-driven copy with subtle product promotion.
  • Connected the flow in Klaviyo, set up triggers, and tested delivery.

Results & Learnings

  • Produced one of my most well-written and design-forward flows.
  • Learned to connect lead magnets to nurturing sequences effectively.
  • Strengthened my approach to balancing copy, design, and timing.
  • Proved the importance of guiding subscribers step-by-step before presenting an offer.

Key takeaway: Thoughtful storytelling, paired with design and timing, transforms a simple pop-up into the start of a customer journey.
