Missionary Connect App – Email Strategy Research

Exploring Mailchimp strategy, large list management, and campaign planning for a real-world SaaS.

Overview

Missionary Connect is a platform designed to help missionaries stay connected with their churches and supporters. I researched and prepared to manage their 5,000+ subscriber list in Mailchimp, focusing on strategies for promoting sales, webinars, and ongoing updates.
Although this project was primarily research and planning, it gave me experience adapting to a new ESP (Mailchimp) while preparing to meet the needs of a real organization.

Process

  • Audited the existing Mailchimp list of 5,000+ contacts.
  • Audited the existing Mailchimp list of 5,000+ contacts.
  • Planned weekly newsletters to maintain supporter engagement.
  • Designed a strategy for sales campaigns and webinar promotions.
  • Explored Mailchimp’s segmentation and automation features for relevance and timing.
  • Prepared workflows to align with organizational goals and stakeholder needs.

Results & Learnings

  • Gained familiarity with Mailchimp workflows and how they compare to Klaviyo.
  • Learned how to approach list management at scale (5,000+ subscribers).
  • Practiced adapting my existing MJML and campaign knowledge to a new platform.
  • Developed confidence in managing real-world client communication needs.

Key takeaway: Every ESP has unique tools, but the fundamentals of segmentation, timing, and messaging remain the same. This project prepared me to confidently handle both small and large-scale email programs.
