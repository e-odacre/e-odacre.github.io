Fable Nest – Spring Campaign Launch

Seasonal email campaign & Shopify buildout for a lifestyle brand.

Overview

I designed and launched a 3-email spring campaign for Fable Nest, a Shopify store selling candles, textiles, décor, and gift bundles. The campaign — Spring Story Begins, Spring Drop, and Last Chance — was coded in MJML, integrated into Klaviyo, and supported by a Shopify pop-up for lead capture. This project demonstrated my ability to manage the full pipeline: from Shopify setup and theme customization (Liquid), to Klaviyo integration and campaign execution, to ensuring emails rendered consistently across devices.

Process

  • Created a Shopify store with product listings and customized theme elements using Liquid.
  • Built and styled a pop-up to capture new subscribers.
  • Planned and wrote a 3-email campaign journey: storytelling → product showcase → final push.
  • Hand-coded responsive MJML templates and exported to Klaviyo.
  • Scheduled and tested campaign delivery, optimizing for both desktop and mobile clients.

Results & Learnings

By comparing performance to a single-email welcome, this project demonstrated:

  • Built a fully functioning Shopify demo store integrated with Klaviyo.
  • Created a multi-touch seasonal campaign showcasing both design and automation skills.
  • Learned the value of balancing storytelling with clear calls-to-action.
  • Gained hands-on experience combining Shopify, Klaviyo, MJML, and Liquid in one project.

What I learned: multi-step flows build stronger trust than one-off campaigns, and timing + segmentation are just as important as design.
